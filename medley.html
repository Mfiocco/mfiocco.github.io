Project Focus

The goal of creating Medley was to redefine how people experience and discover music by merging the visual and auditory senses through an innovative and interactive app. By encouraging users to capture random snapshots of their life, Medley uses AI to analyze these images and generate personalized music playlists inspired by each photo. The app aims to foster creativity and provide a unique way for users to connect with music by giving users the option to share their customized soundscapes with others.
